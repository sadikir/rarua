Who is rebuilding Africa?.

When I hear about Africa, my mind is immedietely taken over by the spirit of enthusiasm, the spirit of hope, the spirit of rebirth. I get occupied by the strong desire to be part of the legion of young ambitious youth pushing for reforms in Africa. Truly, Africa is rising, Afrika is waking up steadly.

The question of who will rebuild Afrika is a complex question yet with an obvious and simple answer. Afrika's rebirth is the responsibility of our maker, but on the other hand the youth of Afrika will have the joy of seeing a rebuilt Afrika, hence it is their responsibility to build the land. A reborn Afrika is like a seed whose fruits will be eaten by our current generation of youth.

Our parents and grand parents lived in an unforgiving system, they were helpless and sought comfort in what could not remove their tears. They tried to build but nothing they built kept standing, because it wasn't built on a firm foundation. All past generations focused on building upon left overs of the colonizers, the oppressors. Our religion, politics, education and finances were all built on the systems our oppressors used to enslave us. Although we celebrate independance, we are like slaves to the systems.Until we break free from the remains of the oppressors, we are not free.

We are so proud of our individual nations when infact we should cherish Afrika as a whole. The coloniser is the one who created these small boxes we call countries in Africa. We exult in the colonizer's languages, educational systems, religions, political philosophies and more... To break free from the shackles of oppression and slavery, we need to divorce their whole systems. These systems are so good at producing slaves. Our parents didn't notice anything but we can notice what is happening. Hence the task to rebuild Africa is in the hands of those who can notice Africa's problems and its none other than the current generation of youth.

Rarua Revolution is a movement of capable, strong willed men and women of all ages who seek to make a lasting impact on our soil. We believe in unity and service to our brotherhood. We accept that the task of rebuilding Afrika is in the hands of our younger generation. Join us in this powerful struggle. God is our ultimate leader.
