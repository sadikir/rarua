Religion and false prophets are a plague in Afrika.

Do you know the men who deliberately lie but it seems the people like them for their lies? We have so many of them in our land. The are so talented at leadership and speaking but they have used their talents for something useless. They have leadership qualities and charisma. These pastors will not save anyone, the false promises will not save anyone. They will push us more and more into bonds.

They are continuing to steal from the poor women and unsuspecting men. Their acts isn't inline with common sense, they are morally wrong and spiritually corrupt. Instead of motivating people to work hard, they are taking away even that which they worked for and in return enlave them to false hope.

Religion is a disease of our land. It is much worse than the ebola epidemic. We need to find solution,otherwise it will keep getting worse. We will produce more and more ignorant families. A family that exults in god but with no knowledge of him. A deluded family with a twisted sense of reality.

We have a plan to win the hearts of these pastors and prophets. They are natural leaders and we love that. They should keep doing their leadership roles but the message should be changed to a more realistic one. All pastors especially the charismatic mega church ones have an understanding of what they are doing. They all know for sure that they are lying and misleading our people. They are just drunk with the power they are given which causes them to perform foolish acts in name of miracles. If all these pastors change their message to a more realistic one, our land will wake up from the sleep so fast. Take an example of Joshua Maponga who was a well known pastor who later kept his leadership role but changed his message from a fictious one to a real one. His change of spirit is something that many can learn from. Maponga did not lose his role as a leader but rather embraced it realistically. He accepted his true calling. He is helping reach even more people with a sound mind. He speaks abusevely of the message he was preaching previously and cherishes in his current audiance. So dear pastors and prophets, you are missing alot. Embrace your leadership role and start leading instead of misleading. You will gain more than you are losing in your current role as a pastor. Turn your passion into something beneficial to our people. Teach more about the real solutions to our challenges and reach more people. You will be remembered for your revolutionary acts.

We have started a cause called Revolutionary Pastors Coalition that will speep up the awakening of our people. This is a move to bring pastors to their true calling. We are working with them while they embrace their true leadership role. Our pastors will preach a more realistic message of hope and freedom instead of white man's fiction. We are working to turn those churces into revolutionary buildings and church goerers enslaved to false hope will become part of our freedom fighters. The white man thought they were enslaving us to false hope, but in reality they were preparing us for the real challenges. If you are a pastor, join our cause of hope. If you are a church goerer push your pastor to join our cause of hope.
