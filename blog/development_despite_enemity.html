How can the black man develop despite world's enemity?.

There is a targeted enemity towards the black man from all over the world. Everyone is against the black man. We are treated completely different from other humans despite beind kind humans. We are systematically despised, hated for no reason. Our developement the cause for fear for other nations. They want us poor, sick, weary and hopeless. So how could we develop when the enemies want us to stay down?

It is our role not to accept the destiny the world has set for us. Our maker knows our future. We need to serve hand in hand with our make to bring our rebirth forth.

The first step to developement is seeking freedom from our slavery, or breaking the shackles, throwing away the chains. We can break the chains by first accepting that we are in chains. There is a hidden oppressive mental slavery that is eating our souls, and a soul is everything. If your soul is sick, your whole body is sick.

Once you accept that there are chains on your neck, you will accept the key to untie the chains. The key is not an actual physical key but it is the truth, wisdom.

There are 5 major areas that has been used to bond the black man to chains. Black man is bound to chains spiritually, financially, politically, socially and education. If we free ourselves from these bonds placed in these areas of our lives, we will have freedom. Mainly spiritually we are in chains, our souls have been beaten down, some of our bodies have been snatched away from us, we are walking around as dead unconscious men. We need spiritual freedom, our souls have to be set free. The spells our enemies have placed on our souls have to be broken. Truly our main problem is spiritual, for we are walking around as blind unwise men because our souls have been blocked from our maker. We are have just started connecting to our true guide. The guardian of modern times hates us so much, he only uses us for doing humiliating things. Once we call upon our maker to come guide us, he will accept our cries for help and will be our guide and leader. The things we should do now is accepting the knowledge of our brothers and not exalting in outside wisdom. Knowledge from our enemies is twisted, written in a foolish language, spell-casting and mixed with many lies.

Not all black men are in same bonds as others. Some by the grace of our maker manages to escape some or all bonds. Accept their wisdom and know that their delight is to set you free. As you do that, know that not everyone who speak the accent of the wise is truly wise, most are just men who are trully in deep bonds and trained to speak the wisdom of our enemies.

Afrika will be free once all our people come to learn that it is our wise men, our inventors, our revellers who will come to free us. Stop exalting in the wisdom of our enemies, it is poisonous. As you wake up, you will come to learn that, what the modern world called wisdom was actually lies, propaganda and organized foolishness. Stop reading their books and online publications, seek wisdom from within. Learn from our own visionaries, from our own revellers, use your own eyes to observe the world, observe yourself you will learn alot. Our own truth is setting us free, Our own maker is setting us free.
