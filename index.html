Who we are.

We are on a mission of total freedom for our people.

Our people are in different bonds and chains they do not know they have. Our mission is to educate our people about these hidden ruthless chains and call them to total freedom. Our people are undergoing a financial, spiritual and social form of attack subjecting them to slavery they can't even notice. Our souls are in deep bonds crying for freedom.

Rarua revolution is a rapidly growing revolutionary movement seeking to wake up our people from the sleep and show them the realities of the modern world. Join the revolution and write your name in the book of freedom fighters. We are fighters!!

Our core beliefs

We strongly believe in the things that bring us together.

Unity

Unity of our souls keep us together. Once you join the movement you become part of a big tree eachone of us is watering. The tree is serving to give our people fruits of total freedom.

Determination

As we plant our seeds, we are determined to keep watering them. We are not just a wave of the times that will pass by, we are here to leave a lasting impact that will last for all times. Our fruits shall be eaten by future generations.

Integrity

We strongly believe in our brotherhood, in the unity of our souls. None of us will seeek deluding comfort anywhere else except in our brotherhood. We are together for all times. Integrity to our brotherhood

Service

We believe in service not to ourselves but to our brotherhood. We are all serving for the betterment of our unity. We are working together to push each one of us to action. We are martyrs to our own unity.
